One of the challenges of classifying EEG is the immense variability in the spectral profile from user to user and even within user, session to session.

Above, you can see that although the data points (3-second windows) are clearly separable within an experiment, the feature space of the clusters changes vastly from session to session.

To address these shortcomings, we tried a variety of signal processing techniques and power normalizations.
